Lexie DeCastro

Multimedia Journalist
KPLC

Lexie DeCastro is a multimedia journalist at KPLC 7News in Lake Charles, La. Born and raised just outside New Orleans, she loves all things Mardi Gras, Jackson Square, and a nice, spicy crawfish boil.

Lexie attended Louisiana State University in Baton Rouge, where she studied broadcast journalism at the Manship School of Mass Communication. She worked as a reporter, anchor, and station manager for Tiger TV student media. In her capstone class, she created and anchored a live show at Festival International de Louisiane in Lafayette before graduating in May 2026.

In her free time, Lexie loves scrapbooking, iced coffee, and playing with her dog. She's excited to bring the “mardi party” to Lake Charles while continuing to serve her beloved home state!
